
GreenWays HR Policies
======================

Effective Date: May 5, 2025
Version: 1.0

1. Introduction
---------------
This document outlines the Human Resource policies of GreenWays. These policies aim to ensure a positive, inclusive, and productive work environment while maintaining compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

2. Equal Employment Opportunity
-------------------------------
GreenWays is an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate against any employee or applicant based on race, color, religion, gender, sexual orientation, national origin, age, disability, or any other protected characteristic.

3. Recruitment and Onboarding
-----------------------------
All recruitment must follow fair and transparent processes. Offers of employment are conditional upon reference checks and, where applicable, background verification.

4. Working Hours
----------------
Standard working hours are from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Employees are entitled to a 1-hour lunch break. Flexible work arrangements may be approved on a case-by-case basis.

5. Attendance and Punctuality
-----------------------------
Regular attendance and punctuality are expected. Absences should be communicated to the reporting manager as early as possible.

6. Leave Policy
---------------
- Paid Annual Leave: 20 days per calendar year.
- Sick Leave: 10 days per year.
- Public Holidays: As per government notifications.
- Parental Leave: Eligible employees are entitled to parental leave in accordance with local laws.

7. Remote Work Policy
---------------------
Employees may request to work remotely. Approval is subject to job responsibilities and managerial discretion.

8. Code of Conduct
------------------
Employees must:
- Act with integrity and professionalism
- Respect colleagues and clients
- Maintain confidentiality
- Avoid conflicts of interest

9. Anti-Harassment Policy
-------------------------
GreenWays maintains a zero-tolerance policy towards harassment. Any incidents should be reported to HR promptly. Investigations will be handled sensitively and confidentially.

10. Performance Management
--------------------------
Performance reviews are conducted semi-annually. Feedback and goal-setting sessions help employees grow and align with business objectives.

11. Disciplinary Procedures
---------------------------
Violations of company policy may lead to disciplinary action, up to and including termination. HR will conduct a fair investigation before any disciplinary steps are taken.

13. Policy Updates
------------------
